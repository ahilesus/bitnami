** Please be patient while the chart is being deployed **

Check the running pods with:

    kubectl -n {{ .Release.Namespace }} get secrets

List all helm chart releases with:

    helm list

To get the value of a secret (eg. postgres-secret):

    kubectl -n {{ .Release.Namespace }} get secret postgres-secret -o jsonpath="{.data.postgres-password}" | base64 --decode

To uninstall Secrets subchart:

    make uninstall NAMESPACE={{ .Release.Namespace }}
